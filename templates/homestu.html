SIDEBAR LINK DESCRIPTIONS

Mentor Acceptance Form

The "Mentor Acceptance Form" link allows you to view and fill out form related to mentor acceptance.
This form is used to confirm and document the acceptance of mentors for the project.

Joining Form

The "Joining Form" link provides access to form required for student or employee onboarding.
This form is used to collect necessary information from new joiners and facilitate their integration into the system.

No Dues Form

The "No Dues Form" link allows you to access and fill out forms related to clearing dues.
This form is used to confirm that all financial or material obligations have been settled before leaving or concluding a program.

Generate Certificate

The "Generate Certificate" link provides access to functionality for creating and issuing certificates.
This feature is used to generate certificates based on the information entered in other forms.
